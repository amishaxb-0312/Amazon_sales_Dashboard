# Amazon_sales_Dashboard
A Power BI dashboard, showing amazon sales data from 2019 to 2022.The dashboard provides insights into sales of past years and the project contributes to learning and finding useful information from the dashboard, to enhance sales.

# Tools and Technologies Used
1. Power BI Desktop
2. Power Query
3. Dax
4. MS Excel

# Dataset
Amazon_Combined_Data.xlsx

# Key KPIs
1. Total YTD Sales
2. Total QTD Sales
3. Total YTD Products sold
4. Total reviews

# Charts and Visuals
1. Sales by month: A line chart depicts the sales per month insights showing higher sales during september to december.
2. Sales by week: column chart depicts the sales by week insights showing an increase in sales as weeks increase.
3. Sales by Product Category: Matrix chart showing YTD sales, % YTD sales and QTD sales of top 6 products.
4. Top 5 Products by YTD Sales: bar chart shows top 5 products by year to date sales.
5. Top 5 Products by YTD Reviews: bar chart shows top 5 products by ytd reviews.

# Insights
1. Nikon Wide Angle shoes topped with most YTD Sales.
2. During September to December YTD Sales peaked which depicts that in the month of festivals, the sales have significantly increased.
3. Looking forward into sales by product category, Men Shoes has the highest % YTD Sales.
